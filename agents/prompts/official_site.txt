OBJECTIVE: Navigate to the target company's official domain and extract structured intelligence.

PRIORITY PAGES (in order):
1. About / Team / Partners pages
2. Portfolio / Investments pages
3. Press releases and announcements
4. Blog posts and thought leadership
5. Careers page (signals growth areas)

EXTRACTION TARGETS:
- Partner names with roles and brief bios
- Investment thesis statements and strategic focus areas
- Recent portfolio companies (last 2 years) with investment stage
- Fund size and LP information if publicly listed
- Key quotes from leadership about strategy or vision

CONSTRAINTS:
- Stay on the official domain and its subdomains only
- Do not follow external links
- If the site requires login, note it and move on
- Prioritize factual data over marketing language
- Extract verbatim quotes where possible (with attribution)

OUTPUT FORMAT: Return 2-5 distinct factual claims, each with a supporting snippet from the page.
